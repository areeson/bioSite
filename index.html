Mikale Wilbon

Mikale S. Wilbon is an athlete at heart. He grew up in Chicago before moving to Lincoln, Ne where he played for the Huskers. For the next six years he worked towards a bachelors in Sociology as well as his masters in Higher Education. With that schooling under his belt, he continued on to work for the Wyoming Cowboys where he is now an Athletic Academic Coordinator.

Beyond just the professional side, Mikale enjoys himself by building great connections with those that are close to him, such as friends and family. He thinks it's always important to stay true to yourself and never minimize who you are for other people. Be authentically yourself. Lastly, he has been implementing fruits, veggies, and fasting into his life as a part of his health and recommends it to stay sharp.
